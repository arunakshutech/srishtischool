About Srishti High School

Established in 1996, Srishti High School has gained immense expertise in offering Higher Secondary School Education Service. We are located in Chengam, Tamil Nadu, and provide Higher Secondary School Education Service to the clients.

Our mission is to foster a positive learning environment where students can grow academically, socially, and emotionally. We are dedicated to providing high-quality education and preparing our students for future success.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a nurturing and challenging educational environment that encourages students to reach their full potential. We aim to develop critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning skills in our students.

Our Vision

We envision a school where every student is inspired to learn, grow, and excel. Our goal is to prepare our students to be responsible global citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Our Values

  • Excellence in Education
  • Integrity and Respect
  • Inclusive and Supportive Community
  • Innovative Learning Environment
  • Commitment to Sustainability

Achievements

Over the years, Srishti High School has been recognized for its excellence in education and numerous achievements:

  • Consistently high academic performance and board exam results
  • State-level awards in sports and extracurricular activities
  • Implementation of innovative teaching methodologies
  • Strong community engagement and support
About Us

Srishti International Matriculation School is a budding school which is practicing International curriculum. It is one of the leading schools in North Chennai. The school has a center approval and can offer Edexcel International Primary, Lower Secondary and IGCSE qualifications to students.

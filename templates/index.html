Welcome to Srishti Matriculation School, Chengam

Srishti places a unique emphasis on personality development. Recognizing the crucial truth that success in the modern world depends on excellence in a chosen field, Srishti seeks to cultivate individual talents and helps the students pursue excellence in their field of interests. Special attention is also paid in Srishti to nurture education in an environment of breezy freedom and unparalleled personal rapport.

Facilities at Our School

Well-Ventilated Classrooms

Our classrooms are designed to provide a healthy and comfortable learning environment.

Separate Classrooms for Boys and Girls

We maintain separate classrooms for boys and girls to ensure a focused learning atmosphere.

Special Training Programs

We offer specialized training in computer skills, silambam (a traditional martial art), yoga, and karate.

Excellence in Spoken English and Handwriting

We provide high-quality training in spoken English and handwriting with experienced instructors.

Monthly Learning Assessments

Regular learning evaluations are conducted, and the results are shared with parents to keep them informed of their child's progress.

Fee Refund for High Scorers in 12th Grade

Students who score above 20 marks in the 12th grade examinations will receive a one-year fee refund.

Fee Concessions for 10th Grade Students

Students in the 10th grade will receive fee concessions based on their public examination scores.

Fee Concessions for Siblings

Families with two or more children enrolled in our school will receive fee concessions.

Support for Single Parent Students

Students with a single parent will receive a 26% fee concession.
